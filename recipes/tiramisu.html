The Best Tiramisu Ever

Description

Indulge in the exquisite layers of velvety mascarpone cheese, coffee-soaked ladyfinger biscuits, and a dusting of rich cocoa powder with this timeless dessert - the classic tiramisu. This Italian delight is a symphony of flavors and textures, combining the boldness of coffee with the creaminess of mascarpone, creating a dessert that's both elegant and comforting.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 3 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 8oz mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 24-30 ladyfinger biscuits

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk the egg yolks and 1/2 cup of sugar until pale and creamy.
  2. Mix in softened mascarpone.
  3. Whip cream and vanilla until soft peaks form; fold into mascarpone mixture.
  4. Dip ladyfingers in coffee, layer in serving dish.
  5. Spread mascarpone mixture over ladyfingers
  6. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.
