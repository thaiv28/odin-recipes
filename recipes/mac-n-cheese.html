Tasty Creamy Mac-n-Cheese

Description

Savor the ultimate comfort food - creamy macaroni and cheese. Velvety cheese sauce blankets tender macaroni, creating a soul-warming dish that's both simple and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 8oz elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni according to package instructions; drain.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter, add flour, and cook briefly.
  3. Gradually whisk in milk; cook until thickened.
  4. Remove from heat; stir in shdredded cheddar until melted
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Serve warm and enjoy!
