Homemade Kaya Toast

Description

Not to be confused with the dinosaur, Kaya is a beloved Southeast Asian delight - an enchanting spread that captures the essence of coconut and pandan. A velvety fusion of eggs, coconut milk, and sugar, delicately infused with the aromatic pandan leaves, creates a luscious, sweet, and fragrant jam. Perfectly paired with toast or as a versatile ingredient, kaya invites a taste of tropical indulgence with every spoonful.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 200g sugar
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • 5 pandan leaves, knotted

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs and sugar in a bowl.
  2. In a saucepan, combine coconut milk, pandan leaves, and a pinch of salt. Heat gently.
  3. Sowly pour the egg mixture into the saucepan, stirring constantly.
  4. Cook on low heat, stirring consistently, until thickened (20-30 minutes).
  5. Remove pandan leaves and let cool.
  6. Spread on toast and enjoy!
